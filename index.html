03 — Experience

Mission Log

Automation & Debugging Engineer
APR 2025 — SEP 2025
◈ Foxconn Technology Group · Chennai, India
  • Assigned to OQC and UMP checkpoints on the iPhone 16 assembly line — two sections with heavy machine usage and zero tolerance for unplanned stops during a shift.
  • Core role was fault diagnosis: tracing sensor drift, actuator timing issues, and communication failures between components — not just clearing alarms and moving on.
  • Monitored UPH figures each shift and made targeted adjustments to motion timing and automation sequences; even incremental cycle parameter changes translated into meaningful daily output gains.
  • Handled installation, calibration, and routine maintenance of robotic arms, conveyor systems, vision inspection cameras, and test fixtures during changeover windows.
  • Started logging fault recurrence data for repeat-offender machines — identifying those patterns eliminated several failures that had been eating into shift time for weeks.
  • Participated in process review sessions with engineers and quality leads, sharing floor-level observations that surfaced issues invisible in upstream metrics.
  • Strictly followed lockout/tagout and equipment clearance procedures — the safety culture was strict and that discipline stuck.
